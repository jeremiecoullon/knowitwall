About

We are a group of fun loving academics who want to capture that pub chat experience for the entire world. Get an insight into the exciting world of academic research with our short 'audio-documentaries' on physics, history, philosophy, and many more subjects.

All audio-docs were written by experts in the subject at hand (PhD students, lecturers, professors) at some of the UK's top universities. They provide first hand accounts of their discipline, and show us what makes it great.

Contribute

Are you an academic or PhD student who is interested in reaching out to the public? Do you feel that your research is too interesting to be bound to academic journals, and that it must be released into the wild?
