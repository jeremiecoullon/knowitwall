Jonathan is a Professor of Physics at UCL, head of the Department of Physics
& Astronomy, and a member of the ATLAS experiment at CERN's Large Hadron Collider.
He writes regularly for ​<i>The Guardian</i>​ and has recently published ​<i>Smashing Physics</i>,
a book​ on his experiences of the discovery of the Higgs boson that was shortlisted
for the 2015 Royal Society Winton Prize for Science Books.
